Entre-Pair-Neurship
Helping entrepreneurs find people with the right skills.

How it works?

If you are a talent with a special skill or an entrepreneur, you can click the link Register my interest/skill. You need to fill in the contact info, your skills/requirements, details, the price range to offer, and You can also price the offer (providing/receiving service). This site won’t provide transaction tools for now. After filling the form, you will receive an email about people matching the skills you need/have.

If you want to browse and select without disclosing your information, you will see the ID, skills, details and price range. If you want to contact anyone, fill in the contact form on the top and bottom of the page. You will need to fill in the ID you want to contact, your contact information, and a convincing message. When it’s submitted, the other person will receive and make the decision of accepting or rejecting the offer. Please be nice and repectful, spam contacts will be filtered.
